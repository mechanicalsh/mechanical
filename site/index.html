How it works

Safety lives on the chip.

The robot runs any model you like for thinking. Mech-1 sits between that model and the motors. It checks each movement against your rules in hardware, and it answers only to those rules. Because the rules live in silicon, they hold steady.

Your model

Bring any AI

Open weights, an API, or your own model. The thinking layer is yours, and you can swap it whenever you like.

The final say

Approved in hardware

Mech-1 gates the motors and clears each movement against your rules. It decides on the chip, in under a millisecond.

Steady rules

Set once, held everywhere

Your rules live in silicon and stay the same across the whole fleet. Every robot follows the version you signed.
