How it works

A gate on the wire to the muscle.

The reasoning model proposes a motion. Before any current reaches a motor, the proposal crosses the core. The core checks it against the constitution and either lets it through or cuts it. Deterministic, on-device, in under a millisecond.

  • [1]

    Independent power path

    The core can drop power to the actuators on its own. Nothing in the model's stack can stop it.

  • [2]

    Deterministic, not learned

    The stop logic is fixed and verifiable. It does not drift, hallucinate, or get fine-tuned away.

  • [3]

    Signs everything

    Each decision is written to a tamper-evident log on the chip's own sealed identity.
