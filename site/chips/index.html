The chip

Mech-1, the safety core.

Mech-1 is a single chip that gives a humanoid robot its boundaries. It sits beside the main processor, watches every movement the AI plans, and clears it against your rules. It runs on certified safety silicon, and you can design it in today.

How it works

Between the model and the motors.

The AI plans a movement. Before any power reaches a motor, the plan crosses Mech-1. The chip checks it against your rules and clears it or holds it. The decision happens on the chip, in real time.

  • [1]

    Its own power path

    Mech-1 controls power to the motors directly. The safety decision stays independent from the AI model.

  • [2]

    Fixed and checkable

    The safety logic is set and formally verified. It behaves the same way every time.

  • [3]

    Signs its work

    Every decision is written to a sealed log on the chip's own identity.
